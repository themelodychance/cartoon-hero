About Melody Chance

Melody Chance is a designer from Toronto, Ontario.

Melody has almost a decade of experience in designing graphics for print and web, including logo design, CDs, posters, icons/UI, booklets, stream overlays and more. She obtained her Bachelor of Music from Humber College in 2019, starting her freelance career a few years earlier designing merch, posters and CDs for her peers. She has also been creating all of her own graphics, editing her own videos and more for many years.

Services:

  1. Print Design
  2. Photo editing
  3. Video editing
